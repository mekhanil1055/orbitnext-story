The story

What this track is about

The AI-Native track is one of the most ambitious in OrbitNext — and one of the longest at 12 months. That length is intentional. Becoming a truly AI-native organisation is not a training programme. It is a cultural shift.

This track is building three things simultaneously: capability in our people (through the SEED Engineers programme and AI skilling), tools and frameworks embedded into delivery, and pilots with clients that demonstrate what AI-first delivery looks like in practice.

The work absorbs the AI Enabled Organisation, Augmenting Tech Teams, and L&D Function major moves. When this track completes, InApp will have transformed from a company that uses AI into a company that is defined by it.

“We are not adding AI to what we do. We are rebuilding what we do around AI.”
