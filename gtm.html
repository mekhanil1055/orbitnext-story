The story

What this track is about

The GTM track is about focus. InApp has deep expertise in Healthcare & Human Services and Construction — two sectors where technology is transforming how organisations serve people and build the world. This track is about going after those sectors with intent.

It absorbs the Strengthening Marketing, Strengthen Account Management, and Customer First major moves. The result is an integrated go-to-market engine: stronger brand presence, larger enterprise deals, and a delivery culture oriented around outcomes rather than outputs.

If SaaSw makes InApp smarter and AI-Native makes InApp stronger, GTM makes InApp visible — and turns that visibility into revenue.

“Domain depth is our competitive advantage. GTM is how we make that advantage visible.”
